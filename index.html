Visit Spring Hill, TN

Spring Hill, Tennessee is a fast-growing, family-friendly town just south of Nashville, known for its rich Civil War history, scenic parks, and welcoming community.

Top Activities in Spring Hill

Explore Historic Sites

Visit the Rippavilla Plantation and the Spring Hill Battlefield to learn about the area's Civil War history.

Enjoy Outdoor Recreation

Spend time at Port Royal State Park or the Spring Hill City Park for hiking, picnicking, and fishing.

Shop and Dine

Explore the local shops and restaurants in downtown Spring Hill for a taste of Southern hospitality.

Attend Local Events:

Check out community events like the annual Spring Hill Christmas Parade or summer festivals.
